DUET SINGING

  • Event type: Duet
  • Event Category: Music
  • Team Size: 2

The following genre are allowed in this competition:

  • Hindi / Bollywood / Folk / Classical / Semi-classical Duet Singing
  • Western / Indo-Western Duet Singing

There shall be only two rounds in this competition and judgment & prizes for both the genre shall be done together as one and not separately.

  • Round 1: Qualifiers / Eliminators
  • Round 2: Grand Finale

NOTE: RULES, REGULATIONS, TIME LIMIT, etc. SHALL STAND SAME FOR ALL THE THREE GENRES UNTILL SPECIFIED!

  • list Rules and Regulations:

    • A participant will be given 6 minutes in total including set up and clearance in each of the round to perform since their name is announced. Minimum performance time should be 3.5 minutes.
    • There shall be elimination at each round. Number of participants to be qualified to next round shall be at the discretion of the Organizers.
    • In the Indo-Western Category, one singer has to perform in an Indian Language while the other in English.
    • In the Western category, both the singers must perform in English language only.
    • In the Indian category, all folk forms, Bollywood Style, Regional Singing, classical, semi-classical and all other Indian forms possible are permitted.
    • Contestant must arrange their own live music or Karaoke. No orchestra support shall be provided by the host University. In case of Live Music, at least 1 Instruments & at most 3 musical instruments must be used.
    • No performance shall be permitted without Instruments or Karoake.
    • In case of karaoke, please carry it in Pen Drives only. Use of Mobile Phones / Laptops / ipods, etc. for playing tracks/karaoke shall not be permitted.
