  • list Rules and Regulations:

    Round 1:Essay Writing

    • Participants will be given 25 minutes to write an essay on one of the 3 topics that shall be announced on the spot.
    • Number of words should not exceed 500 words and must be a minimum of 250 words.

    Round 2:Creative Writing (Can be a prose or a poetry or a combination of both)

    • Participants will be given 30 minutes to write a CREATIVE ARTICLE which can be in the form of a prose or a poetry or a combination of both the essay.
    • An abstract topic will be given on the spot for creative writing.

    Round 3:Story Writing based on Key words

    • In this 5-10 keywords shall be given and based on those keywords a short story has to be developed by the contestant which should not exceed 350 words.
    • Participants will be given 30 minutes to write the essay.

    Content, Grammar and Vocabulary, Word Limit Adherence, Appropriateness & Overall Impact
