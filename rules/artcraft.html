ART &CRAFT MARATHON

(Greeting Cards Making, Photo Frame Design & Artificial Bouquet Design)

  • Event type: By choice
  • Event Category: Fine Arts
  • Team Size: 1-4

The Arts & Craft Marathon is a series of three events that shall be conducted in one go in a span of 3 hours (180 Minutes).

Three products is to be prepared by an individual or a group of 1 to 4 students. The products are:

  • Hand-made Greeting Cards
    • The Maximum dimension of the card permitted is 21 cm x 15 cmeither in portrait or landscape.
    • The card should not have more than one fold.
    • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
    • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
    • Theme for which the card is to be made shall be given by the organizers on the spot.
  • Photo frame Design
    • The Maximum dimension of the photo frame permitted is 15 cm x 10 cm either in portrait or landscape.
    • The photo frame should have stand and hanger.
    • Contestants are free to bring any material of their choice.
    • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
    • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
    • Theme for which the photo frame is to be made shall be given by the organizers on the spot.
  • Artificial Bouquet Design
    • The Maximum dimension of the Bouquet is 45 cm x 25 cm x 15 cm.
    • The Bouquet should be well wrapped so that it can be ready for presentation to anyone.
    • Contestants are free to bring any material of their choice.
    • Anything other than any natural material such as flowers, leaves, etc. can be used.
    • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
    • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
    • Theme for which the phot frame is to be made shall be given by the organizers on the spot.

Note:

  • Team composition is at the discretion of the team and can compose of 1 to 4 members.
  • There is no sectional timing. A cumulative timing of 180 minutes shall be given.
  • On the completion of 180 minutes, teams shall be required to submit all the three products, irrespective of what the level of completion of the product is.
  • All products shall be judged individually and then result shall be declared on the summation of individual works.
