THE GENERAL QUIZ

As the name suggests General quiz is usually and shall be beyond the horizon. However the major emphasis shall be on current affairs & happenings, general knowledge & things that you usually observe around yourself.

  • Event type: Group
  • Event Category: Literary
  • Team Size: 3
  • There will be two rounds in this competition:
    • Written Prelims (eliminator)
    • Mains (Finale)

    NOTE: RULES, REGULATIONS, TIME LIMIT, etc. SHALL STAND SAME FOR ALL THE THREE GENRES UNTILL SPECIFIED!

Decision in case of incomplete or partially correct answers or spelling mistakes in answers shall lie at the discretion of the quiz master.
      • In case the qualifying team is not present at the time of announcement of prelims results, privilege shall be given to the next team depending on the score and availability.       • The details of various rounds and their marking scheme of the mains, shall be announced before the start of the mains.
