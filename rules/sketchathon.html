Sketchathon

  • Event type: By Choice
  • Event Category: Fine Arts
  • Team Size: 3

(Greeting Cards Making, Photo Frame Design & Artificial Bouquet Design)

The Sketchan is a painting oriented marathon competition in which a team or an individual is expected to:

  • Charcoal Potrait Painting
    • Charcoalism is a charcoal portrait making in which with the help of charcoal one has to make portrait on a given theme.
    • Use of mobile phones or any electrical gadgets are strictly prohibited.
    • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
    • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
  • On the Spot Painting
    • Painting can be done in oil, water, poster or pastel colours.
    • Candidate shall bring their own material like brushes, paints etc. Only thepaper/ sheet will be provided by the host institute.
    • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
    • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
    • Use of mobile phones or any electrical gadgets are strictly prohibited.
  • VIBGYOR(Color Mixing)
    • It is a color mixing competition in which the contestants have to form certain pattern or images using the given colors by using your fingers & palms instead of paint brushes. The contestants shall be provided with a 7 water color set using which they shall be required to make a meaningful at work (painting) based on the theme that shall be given on the spot on the day of event.
    • Use of brushes or any other painting tool/instruments are not allowed and shall lead to disqualification. Work has to be done by hand only.
    • Use of mobile phones or any electrical gadgets are strictly prohibited.
    • Theme shall be given on the spot.
    • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
    • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
