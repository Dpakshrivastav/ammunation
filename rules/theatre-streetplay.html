  • list Rules and Regulations:

    • The main language may be either Hindi or English or a combination of the two. Use of regional languages is allowed but must be reduced to a minimum.
    • The script can be original or an adaptation.
    • maximum of 15 minutes shall be given including setup and clearance. Teams exceeding time limit shall be penalized. Your time starts counting as soon as you step on the stage, even though for arranging props.
    • Performance should be confined within the Nukkad area, as defined by the organizers.
    • No technical support will be provided. Teams are allowed to use Dholak, Flute or any other music instrument of their choice, but usage of mikes, lights or other electrical instruments is not allowed.
    • No props will be provided. However teams may use their own props.
    • Any form of obscenity may result in penalty at the discretion of Judges. It may range from point deduction to disqualification.
    • An individual can be a part of more than one team in this competition. However the organizers shall not be responsible if the team in which the individual is participating gets subsequent slot of performance.
    • Decisions of the judges and the organizers shall be final and binding to all teams.
