ELOCUTION: ENGLISH & HINDI

  • Event type: Solo
  • Event Category: Literary
  • Team Size: 1

THIS COMPETITION IS ORGANIZED UNDER TWO LANGUAGES: ENGLISH and HINDI, SEPARATELY. ONE INDIVIDUAL CAN PARTICIPATE IN BOTH, HOWEVER THE INDIVIDUAL CANNOT SWITCH LANGUAGES IN BETWEEN THE COMPETITION.

  • list Rules and Regulations:

    Round 1:Elocution

    • Speech has to be presented either in English or Hindi throughout the competition.
    • Time Limit is 4 minutes for each participant. However, the participants may be stopped in between by the judges.
    • The first bell for each speaker shall ring at 3 minutes followed by a warning bell at 3.45 minutes after which the speaker shall have 15 seconds to wind up. Individuals exceeding the permissible time limit of 4 minutes shall be disqualified.
    • A maximum of 8-10 contestants shall qualify to the next round.

    Round 2:Turn Coat

    • Topic shall be given on the spot. However a preparation time of 5 minutes shall be given to each contestant.
    • Language permitted is either English or Hindi.
    • Participant has to speak on the topic for a minimum of 2 minutes. For the first half (1 minute) he/she will speak for the motion and in the next half (1 minute) he/she will speak against the motion. A bell will indicate the signal for transition, at the culmination of the first minute.
    • Individuals exceeding the permissible time limit of 2 minutes shall be disqualified.
    • Reading from paper/other resources such as mobile phone/ tabs etc. is not permissible any shall lead to immediate disqualification.
    • The winner will be decided by cumulative marks of both the rounds.
    • The decision of the judging panel will be final and binding.

    Round 3:Creative Speaking

    • Topic shall be given on the spot. The topic shall be one with open ended dimensions. No preparation time shall be given.
    • Language permitted is either English or Hindi.
    • Participant has to speak on the topic for a minimum of 1.5 minutes and a maximum of 3 minutes.
    • The first bell for each speaker shall ring at 2 minutes followed by a warning bell at 2.45 minutes after which the speaker shall have 15 seconds to wind up. Individuals exceeding the permissible time limit of 3 minutes shall be disqualified.
    • Reading from paper/other resources such as mobile phone/ tabs etc. is not permissible any shall lead to immediate disqualification.
    • The winner will be decided by cumulative marks of both the rounds.
    • The decision of the judging panel will be final and binding.
