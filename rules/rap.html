Rap SINGING

  • Event type: Solo
  • Event Category: Music
  • Team Size: 1(+2 Beat Boxers)

Two genre are permitted in this competition. However, participants are required to note that the judgement & award of both the genre shall be under the same event.

  • Western (Only English)
  • Indian (Punjabi / Haryanvi / Hindi)

There shall be two rounds in this competition:

  • Eliminator
  • Finale

NOTE: RULES, REGULATIONS & TIME LIMIT SHALL STAND SAME FOR BOTH ROUNDS.

  • list Rules and Regulations:

    • Genre once chosen cannot be changed.
    • The rap me original or an adaptation, however, additional points shall be awarded for originality.
    • The rap must be Hindi and / or Punjabi.
    • A maximum of 5 minutes shall be given for performance for every contestant. Exceeding time limit will lead to disqualification. Minimum performance time should be 3 minutes.
    • In the allotted five minutes a performer may perform more than one rap.
    • 2 printed copies of lyrics must be submitted to the organizers before the performance.
    • Contestants have to perform on produced beats or live beats through Beat Boxers. In either case, the contestants have to arrange the same.
    • No performance shall be permitted without beats or beat boxer.
    • Performers must perform either karaoke or with beat boxers (maximum 2).
    • The lyrics must not be provocative or vulgar and any attempt of provoking the mass will not be tolerated and in this case, the participant shall be disqualified.
    • The organizers/Judges hold the right to interrupt or drop the performance any time regardless of the time limit on the basis of unacceptable performance level and/or breach of rules of the event.
    • The organizers hold the rights to enforce changes in the matter depending upon the suitability to the university without Prior Notice.
    • All Decisions by the Judges are Final.

  • gavel Judging Criteria

    Originality / matching the set limit by original performer, Word Play, Variations, and Performance & Overall Impact
