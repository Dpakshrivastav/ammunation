    Round 1: Tag It:Write suitable tag lines

    • 4 Pictures, 3 Products and 3 Movie posters shall be given and you have to write suitable taglines not exceeding 8-12 Words

    • Time duration of 20 Minutes shall be granted.

    • 5-10 Key words (English and Hindi) shall be provided based on which one has to create a NANO TALE.
    • At least 5 Nano tales shall to be created not exceeding 25 Words each.
    • Language can be either English or Hindi or even a combination of both
    • Time duration of 20 Minutes shall be granted
  • judge Judging Criteria
