The Paperwork marathon

Paper Dress Designing, Crown Designing & Paper based ornament designing

  • Event type: By Choice
  • Event Category: Fine Arts
  • Team Size: 1-4

The paperwork Marathon is a series of three events that shall be conducted in one go in a span of 3 hours (180 Minutes).

Three products is to be prepared by an individual or a group of 1 to 4 students. The products are:

  • Paper-dress design
    • Type and dimension of dress to be designed shall be given on the spot. Example: A One-piece dress is to be designed for a Lady of height 5 ft. 6 in with waist size 30 in.
    • Dress has to be designed using Newspaper and Magazine cut outs only in case color is required. Use of papers of any other type is strictly prohibited.
    • Contestants are free to bring any material of their choice.
    • The dress should be tested on a lady and hence the dress should be in a situation that it can fit an average 5ft 6 in lady.
    • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
    • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
  • crown Designing
    • Two crowns one each for a King and a Queen is to be prepared.
    • Any material such as paper, wires, polysters, etc. can be used.
    • Contestants are free to bring any material of their choice.
    • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
    • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
    • Theme for which the photo frame is to be made shall be given by the organizers on the spot.
  • Paper Based Ornament
    • An Ornament Set containing of an Ear-ring, necklace and a ring is to be prepared.
    • Any material such as paper, wires, polysters, etc. can be used.
    • Contestants are free to bring any material of their choice.
    • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
    • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
    • Theme for which the phot frame is to be made shall be given by the organizers on the spot.
