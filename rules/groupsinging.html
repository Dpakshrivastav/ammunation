SOLO SINGING COMPTITIONS

  • Event type: Group
  • Event Category: Music
  • Team Size: 4-8

This competition shall be open to the following genres, however, it is to be noted that all genre shall be evaluated under the same parameter and in the same competition; however, in case the competition level is very high, prizes may be awarded separately too, but that shall be at the discretion of the organizers.

  • Folk Group Singing
  • Western Group Singing
  • Indo-Western Group Singing
  • Group Devotional Singing
  • Opera, etc.

There shall be two rounds in this competition:

  • Eliminator
  • Grand Finale

  • list Rules and Regulations:

    • The choice of genre is independent, however, all genre shall be evaluated under one competition and same judging parameters.
    • The performance can be on Live Music or Karaoke or Pre-recorded Music. It is to be noted that use DRUM SETS shall not be permitted under this category, however instruments such as Congo, Dholak and all other instruments may be used.
    • In case performing Live Music, teams must use a minimum of 2 Instruments and a maximum of 5 Instruments
    • More than one song can be sung within the time limit.
    • Participants shall be given 10 minutes to perform including set up and clearance. Time starts counting as soon as you step on the stage even if for arranging instrument. Minimum performance time should be 5 minutes.
    • An individual can be a part of more than one team in this competition. However the organizers shall not be responsible if the team in which the individual is participating gets subsequent slot of performance.
    • The organizers/Judges reserves the right to interrupt or drop the performance any time regardless of the time limit on the basis of unacceptable performance level and/or breach of rules of the event.
    • The organizers hold the rights to enforce changes in the matter depending upon the suitability to the university without prior notice.
