Alpana: Rangoli Marathon

(Colour based rangoli& food grain based rangoli)

  • Event type: Solo/Duet
  • Event Category: Fine Arts
  • Team Size: 1-2

In this competition two types of Rangoli is to be designed by the contestants.

The first type of rangoli has to be made with Rangoli Colors, while the second type of rangoli is to be made using food grains such as Pulse (Dal), Rice, Soozi, etc.

Following shall be the rules and regulation:p>

  • Colored based rangoli has to be circular in shape. It must have a 3D effect.
  • Food grain based rangoli must be rectangular or square in shape.
  • The minimum dimensions of each Rangoli cannot be bigger than 1.5metre by 1.5metre.
  • Total time for making both the Rangoli shall be 180 minutes.

Note:

  • Team composition is at the discretion of the team and can compose of 1 to 2 members.
  • There is no sectional timing. A cumulative timing of 180 minutes shall be given.
  • On the completion of 180 minutes, teams shall be required to submit both the rangoli for evaluation irrespective of what the level of completion of the rangoliis.
  • Both the rangolishall be judged individually and then result shall be declared on the summation of individual works.
