POETRY COMPOSITION CUM RECITATION

  • Event type: Solo
  • Event Category: Literary
  • Team Size: 1

This competition shall be conducted separately for the following categories:

  1. English
  2. Hindi

There are two rounds in this competition:

Round 1:Poetry Writing

Round 2:Poetry Recitation

The winner shall be decided on the combined score of both rounds. Only a limited number of contestants shall be qualified from Round 1 to Round 2.

  • list Rules and Regulations:

    • It is an individual event.
    • Poems have to be presented either in English or Hindi only.
    • In first round, participants will be given 45 minutes to write the poem.
    • 5 Topics will be given on the spot for poem writing and participants will have to choose one topic from them.
    • In second round, each participant will have to recite their own poem which he/she has written on the spot. Time Limit is 5 minutes for each participant. However, the participants may be stopped in between by the judges if they find anything which is against the policy.
    • All Decisions by the judges would be considered Final.
    • There should be no vulgarity in the performance of any type, verbal or action.

  • gavel Judging Criteria

    Delivery of poem, Voice modulations, Content & theme, Rhyme and Expressions & overall impact
