SOLO DANCE COMPETITIONS

  • Event type: Solo
  • Event Category: Dance
  • Team Size: 1

The following genre are permitted under this competition and shall be judged and award separately:

  • Classical / Semi-classical
  • Folk Dance(Any Indian /International Folk Dance)
  • Indian Bollywood Style Dance / Indian Contemporary
  • Western Free Style Dance / Western Contemporary

There shall be three rounds in this competition.

  • Round 1: Eliminator
  • Round 2: Qualifier
  • Round 3: Grand Finale

NOTE: RULES, REGULATIONS, TIME LIMIT, etc. SHALL STAND SAME FOR ALL THE THREE GENRES UNTILL SPECIFIED!

  • list Rules and Regulations:

    • All performances are to be done on music tracks only.
    • In Classical / Semi-classical competition, a contestant is required to any dance styles such as Kathak, Bharatnatyam, Kathakali, Mohiniattam, Odissi, Kuchipudi, Manipuri, attriya, Semi classical dance style and their variations.
    • In the Folk Category, Participants can perform any type of folk dances. All Indian as well as Non-Indian Folk Dances are permissible in this competition.
    • In Western / Free Style / Bollywood Solo Dance Category, any dance form except classical, semi-classical & folk dance is to be performed. Some of the dance forms that can be performed are Hip-Hop, Break dance, Jazz, Jive, Tap dance, Robot, Locking, Popping, Rock & Roll, Ballet, Contemporary, all International Dance Forms and their variations. Contestants may perform International - non-folk Dance forms as well.
    • In Indian Bollywood Style a contestant is expected to perform all those cool yet unclassified dance form that the Bollywood sees.
    • A maximum of 5 minutes performance time is provided including setup and clearance. Your time starts counting as soon as you step on the stage, even though for arranging props.Minimum performance time should be 3 minutes.
    • Participants are required to perform on pre-recorded music tracks only.
    • Participants are required to get their tracks in 2 pen drives (carrying tracks in mobile phones, laptops and any other electronic gadgets shall not be permitted).
    • Vulgarity/Obscenity or provocative acts will not be tolerated.
    • Songs which are classified as Item Songs shall not be permitted to be performed. If the judges / organizers feel, deems a particular song as Item Song or vulgar or provoking or having foul words by any standard, their performance may be stopped and the contestant shall be disqualified from the competition.
    • Teams using props if any must bring their own props and shall be responsible for arrangement and clearance of props within the given time. The organizers must not be asked for any help for the same.
