  • list Rules and Regulations:

    • It is a team event and each team will be comprising of two speakers.
    • There will be 2 rounds in the competition and the team qualifying through all rounds and getting maximum cumulative points (i.e. summation of all the two rounds) shall win.

    Round 1:Elimination

    • Each team will be given 8 minutes to speak (4 minutes to each speaker).
    • First speaker has to speak for the motion and the other will speak against it.
    • The first bell for each speaker shall ring at 3 minutes followed by a warning bell at 3.45 minutes after which the speaker shall have 15 seconds to wind up. Speaker exceeding the permissible time limit of 4 minutes shall lead to the disqualification of the entire team.
    • Reading from paper/other resources such as mobile phone/ tabs etc. in any round is not permissible any shall lead to immediate disqualification.
    • A maximum of 4-5teams will qualify for Round 2.
    • The decision of the judges shall be final & binding.

    Round 2:Elimination

    • Each team will be given 6 minutes to speak (3 minutes to each speaker).
    • First speaker has to speak for the motion and the other will speak against it.
    • The first bell for each speaker shall ring at 2 minutes followed by a warning bell at 2.45 minutes after which the speaker shall have 15 seconds to wind up. Speaker exceeding the permissible time limit of 3 minutes shall lead to the disqualification of the entire team.
    • Reading from paper/other resources such as mobile phone/ tabs etc. in any round is not permissible any shall lead to immediate disqualification.
    • After every speaker has spoken there will be a rebuttal. He /She will have to face one question from the fellow participants or audience.
    • Cross-questioning is against the rules.
    • The decision of the judges shall be final & binding.
  • gavel Judging Criteria
