  • list Rules and Regulations:
    • Face Painting
      • The person on whose face, the painting shall be done must be a part of the team and must be arranged by the participating team.
      • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
      • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
      • Theme will be provided on the spot.
    • Tattoo Designing
      • The person on whose body, the tattoo shall be designed must be a part of the team and must be arranged by the participating team.
      • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
      • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
      • Theme will be provided on the spot.
    • Mehndi Designing
      • Designing should be done on only one of the two hands. The design style should be Indian and not Mighlai.
      • The person on whose body, the tattoo shall be designed must be a part of the team and must be arranged by the participating team.
      • Mehndi Cone and other required materials are to be arranged the participating team.
      • Anything other than any natural material such as flowers, leaves, etc. can be used.
      • No material / tools / equipment shall be provided by the organizer. All materials to be used are to be arranged by the contestants on their own expenses and choices.
      • Credit / Marks shall be given for MINIMUM RESOURCE utility giving the best look.
      • Theme will be provided on the spot.
