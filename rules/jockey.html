The Jockey Hunt: RJ / VJ / Stage Anchor Hunt

  • Event type: Solo
  • Event Category: Literary
  • Team Size: 1

A unique public speaking contest covering all three major aspects: RJing, VJing and Stage Anchoring! Language of Speaking can be English / Hindi or even a combination of both.

There will be three rounds:

Round 1:VJing

Round 2:Stage Anchoring

Round 3:RJing

NOTE:This event is completely an extempore based event. Hence detailed description shall be provided on the spot on the day of event. More than 3 round may be required. All round may or may not be conducted on the same day.
