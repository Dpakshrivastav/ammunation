SHORT FILM /DOCUMENTARY MAKING COMPETITION

  • Event type: Group
  • Event Category: Photography
  • Team Size: 1-15

It is a short film/documentary making competition in which a group of students are required to make an entertainment or fiction based feature film or a documentary not exceeding duration of 15 minutes.
It has two categories:

  • Entertainment / Fiction
  • Documentary /Social Message

It must be noted that all the technical requirement of the film such as Direction, Lead Acting, Cinematography, Camera Work, Screen Play, Background Score, etc. must be fulfilled by the students only and no professional support should be sought. In case any professional support is taken, it shall lead to immediate disqualification from the event.

  • list Rules and Regulations:

    • Your short film should not be longer than 15 minutes and not shorter than 3 minutes.
    • The entry must be the original work of the student(s).
    • The production team size should not exceed 15 and shall not be less than 5 including all actors and actresses (scenes where the crowd is involved is acceptable without being counted in the production team).
    • Limited use of copyrighted material will be permitted in the entry so long as it is a lawful or "fair" use, and so long as such use does not overwhelm the contribution of the student(s).
    • Please make sure that your finished short film is under 1.5 GB in size and that it is in one of the following video formats: .AVI/ .MOV /.MPG/ .MPG2/ .MPEG2 /.MPEG4 /.MP4/ .WMV and bring it in HDD or PD only.
    • The host University reserves the right to use the photographs submitted for evaluation for any publication purpose of the University.
