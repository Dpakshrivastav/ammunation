DUET DANCE COMPETITIONS

  • Event type: Duet
  • Event Category: Dance
  • Team Size: 2(Both Male/both female/One Oale One Female)

The following genre are permitted under this competition and shall be judged and award separately:

  • Classical / Semi-classical
  • Folk Dance(Any Indian /International Folk Dance)
  • Indian Bollywood Style Dance / Indian Contemporary
  • Western Free Style Dance / Western Contemporary

There shall be two rounds in this competition.

  • Round 1: Eliminator
  • Round 2: Finale

NOTE: RULES, REGULATIONS, TIME LIMIT, etc. SHALL STAND SAME FOR ALL THE THREE GENRES UNTILL SPECIFIED!

  • list Rules and Regulations:

    • A maximum of 5 minutes performance time is provided including setup and clearance. Your time starts counting as soon as you step on the stage, even though for arranging props.Minimum performance time should be 3.5 minutes.
    • Participants are required to get their tracks in 2 pen drives (carrying tracks in mobile phones, laptops and any other electronic gadgets shall not be permitted).
    • Songs which are classified as Item Songs shall not be permitted to be performed. If the judges / organizers feel, deems a particular song as Item Song or vulgar or provoking or having foul words by any standard, their performance may be stopped and the contestant shall be disqualified from the competition.
    • Vulgarity or provocative acts will not be tolerated.
    • Teams using props if any must bring their own props and shall be responsible for arrangement and clearance of props within the given time. The organizers must not be asked for any help for the same.
    • An individual can be a part of more than one team in this competition. However the organizers shall not be responsible if the team in which the individual is participating gets subsequent slot of performance.
    • The organizers hold the right to interrupt or drop your performance any time, regardless of the time limit, on the basis of indecent track, dance steps, costumes, misuse of props, etc.
