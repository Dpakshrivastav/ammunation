SOLO SINGING COMPTITIONS

  • Event type: Solo
  • Event Category: Music
  • Team Size: 1

Solo Singing Competitions shall be organized separately in four genre of competition which shall be conducted and judged separately. The genres are:

  • Bollywood Style: Solo Singing
  • Light Indian Vocal: Classical / Sufi / Bhakti Geet / Arziyaan / Ardaas / etc.
  • Folk / Regional Style: Solo Singing (Any folk form is permitted)
  • Western / English: Solo Singing

There shall be three rounds in this competition.

  • Round 1: Eliminator
  • Round 2: Qualifier
  • Round 3: Grand Finale

NOTE: RULES, REGULATIONS, TIME LIMIT, etc. SHALL STAND SAME FOR ALL THE THREE GENRES UNTILL SPECIFIED!

  • list Rules and Regulations:

    • A participant will be given 4 minutes in total including set up and clearance in each of the round to perform since their name is announced. Minimum performance time should be 2.5 minutes.
    • There shall be elimination at each round. Number of participants to be qualified to next round shall be at the discretion of the Organizers.
    • A participant shall compete in more than one genre, but has to register separately for both the genres. A genre once selected shall remain same throughout the competition. It means, mixture of two genre shall not be permitted.
    • Contestant must arrange their own live music or Karaoke. No orchestra support shall be provided by the host University. In case of Live Music, at least 1 Instruments & at most 3 musical instruments must be used.
    • No performance shall be permitted without Instruments or Karoake.
    • In case of karaoke, please carry it in Pen Drives only. Use of Mobile Phones / Laptops / ipods, etc. for playing tracks/karaoke shall not be permitted.
    • Songs which are classified as Item Songs shall not be permitted to be performed. If the judges / organizers feel, deems a particular song as Item Song or vulgar or provoking or having foul words by any standard, their performance may be stopped and the contestant shall be disqualified from the competition.
    • The organizers/Judges reserves the right to interrupt or drop the performance any time regardless of the time limit on the basis of unacceptable performance level and/or breach of rules of the event.
    • The organizers hold the rights to enforce changes in the matter depending upon the suitability to the university without prior notice.
