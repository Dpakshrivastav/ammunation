SPORTS & ENTERTAINMENT QUIZ

  • Event type: Group
  • Event Category: Literary
  • Team Size: 2-3

In the Sports & Entertainment Quiz, Questions can be asked from the following areas and related (extended) fields:

  • Sports & Games
  • Records, Achievements & Statistics
  • Sports person, brand endorsement & media
  • Olympics, Common Wealth Games, Asian Games, World Cups & other such tournaments and championships
  • Hollywood, Bollywood, Tollywood (South Indian Film Industry), Pollywood (Punjabi Film Industry)
  • Awards, trends, celebrities and events
  • Popular programs telecasted on TV channels (sitcoms, drama and reality)
  • Fictional characters, cartoons and super heroes
  • Music & Dance icons
  • Famous performers, trivia facts etc.

Note: Questions are not strictly bound to these areas & questions may go beyond these boundaries

  • list Rules and Regulations:

    • There will be two rounds in this competition:
      • Written Prelims (eliminator)
      • Mains (Finale)
    • This is a group event and there must be a minimum of two and a maximum of three members in each team in each of the rounds
    • Top 4 or 6 teams from the prelims shall qualify to the main round depending upon the scores in the prelims. In case of tie in the prelims, the qualifying team shall be decided on the basis of maximum correctly answered *marked questions in the prelims.
    • There shall be 15-25 questions in the prelims each carrying 1 mark. There shall be some specific 3-8 *marked questions. There shall be no negative marking for unanswered questions or incorrect answers. Decision in case of incomplete or partially correct answers or spelling mistakes in answers shall lie at the discretion of the quiz master.
    • In case the qualifying team is not present at the time of announcement of prelims results, privilege shall be given to the next team depending on the score and availability. It is therefore mandatory for the all teams to be present at the specified time and venue for the announcements of prelims results.
    • The details of various rounds and their marking scheme of the mains, shall be announced before the start of the mains.
    • The quiz master shall be the only person responsible and decider of the question that can be placed under this quiz. No one else can claim against the domain of the question asked under this category.
    • The decision of the quiz master shall be final and bounding under all circumstances in all rounds.
    • Carrying reading/writing materials, cell phones, tablets, etc. at the venue of the prelims or mains while attending it shall lead to disqualification of the team.
    • Any attempt of the questions answered by your friends/ your college mates or any other resource person during the mains by shouting the answers out, may lead to losing points/losing that particular question or even to disqualification of the team.
